1 Product Vision

TalkLearn transforms any company's onboarding content into an interactive, personalized learning experience delivered through the channels new hires already use. It's not an LMS. It's a learning companion — a buddy that turns boring policies into engaging conversations, static documents into dynamic podcasts, and compliance manuals into interactive WhatsApp sessions. Every new hire gets their own AI learning partner who adapts to their pace, tests their understanding, and makes the first 90 days feel like a guided adventure rather than an information dump.

The One-Liner

"Upload your content. We turn it into a learning experience. Delivered by an AI buddy your new hires will actually want to talk to."

Strategic Positioning

TalkLearn extends Talkpush's platform from recruitment automation into onboarding training — owning the critical "first 90 days" that determine whether a new hire stays or leaves. This is a natural extension of the Talkpush Conversational AI that already engages candidates via WhatsApp, Messenger, SMS, and voice.

🎯
The Big Bet: Companies spend $6,500+ per trainee on classroom-based onboarding in BPO environments. TalkLearn can reduce this by 60-70% while achieving higher knowledge retention (85%+ vs. 25% for static courses) by delivering training through conversational AI on WhatsApp — a channel with 98% open rates and 90% response rates.

🤖 Pillar 2: The AI Onboarding Buddy

Every new hire gets a personal AI companion that acts as their friendly guide throughout the onboarding journey. It's not a chatbot that answers FAQs — it's a learning partner that remembers context, adapts to pace, celebrates progress, and genuinely cares about the new hire's success.

Buddy Personality Traits

  • Warm & Encouraging: Celebrates milestones, uses positive reinforcement
  • Patient & Adaptive: Explains concepts differently when someone doesn't understand
  • Contextually Aware: Knows the employee's role, team, start date, and learning progress
  • Proactively Helpful: Sends morning check-ins, suggests next lessons, reminds about deadlines
  • Honest & Transparent: Escalates to a human when the question is beyond its scope
  • Culturally Aware: Adapts tone and style to the company's culture and the employee's language

The WhatsApp Training Experience

A document about "Customer Escalation Procedures" becomes a 5-message conversation over 2 days:

  • Message 1: "Hey Maria! Today let's learn about handling customer escalations. There are 3 types. Want me to walk you through them?" [Yes / Later]
  • Message 2: Explains Type 1 with a scenario + asks: "What would you do?" [Multiple choice buttons]
  • Message 3: Provides feedback on answer + explains correct approach + audio summary
  • Message 4: Next day: "Morning! Quick recap from yesterday — can you remember the 3 types of escalation?" [Open text response]
  • Message 5: AI evaluates response, reinforces gaps, sends completion badge

Real-time visibility into every new hire's learning journey — from content completion to genuine knowledge retention.

Tracking Dimensions

✅

Completion Tracking

Module-by-module progress, time spent, engagement rate per content type. Not just "opened" but "actively engaged."

🧠

Knowledge Retention

Spaced repetition quizzes measure real understanding over time, not just immediate recall. Tracks knowledge decay curves.

🕑

Time-to-Competency

Measures when a new hire reaches proficiency benchmarks for their role. Predicts ramp-up timeline based on learning velocity.

🙂

Sentiment & Engagement

AI analyzes response patterns and buddy interactions to detect confusion, frustration, or disengagement early.

👥

Cohort Analytics

Compare training effectiveness across hiring cohorts, locations, departments, and content formats.

⚠️

Risk Alerts

Early warning system identifies new hires falling behind or showing signs of disengagement before it's too late.

🔒 Pillar 5: Compliance & Certification Engine

Automated compliance training with audit-ready tracking — critical for BPO, financial services, and healthcare clients.

Compliance Features

  • Mandatory Module Enforcement: Block advancement until compliance modules are passed
  • Digital Signatures: Employees acknowledge policy understanding with e-signatures (TalkSign integration)
  • Audit Trail: Complete log of who completed what, when, scores, and attempts
  • Expiration & Recertification: Automatic reminders when certifications expire
  • Regulatory Templates: Pre-built modules for TCPA, PCI-DSS, HIPAA, GDPR, SOX

Certification System

  • Digital Badges: Awarded upon module completion — shareable on LinkedIn
  • Skill Certificates: PDF certificates with QR verification codes
  • Proficiency Levels: Bronze, Silver, Gold based on assessment scores
  • Manager Sign-Off: Optional human verification step before certification
  • Continuous Validation: Periodic re-checks to ensure knowledge retention

12 Pricing Strategy

Pricing Model: Per New Hire Trained

Aligns cost with value delivered. Companies pay based on volume of new hires trained through TalkLearn, not per seat or per month.

TierVolumePrice per HireIncludes
Starter Up to 100 hires/month $25-35 per hire Content Studio, AI Buddy (WhatsApp), Basic Analytics, 5 content uploads/month
Professional 100-500 hires/month $15-25 per hire Everything in Starter + Podcasts, Video Lessons, Advanced Analytics, Learning Paths, Unlimited uploads
Enterprise 500+ hires/month $8-15 per hire Everything in Professional + Voice Roleplay (Sam), Compliance Engine, Certifications, Simulations, Custom Integrations, Dedicated CSM

Pricing Justification

💰
ROI Case: A BPO spending $6,500/trainee on 6-week classroom training could reduce this to $2,500-3,000 using TalkLearn (training in 2-3 weeks, no classroom costs, no trainer salaries for basic content). At $15/hire for 500 hires/month, TalkLearn costs $7,500/month — saving $1.75M annually while improving knowledge retention from 25% to 80%+.

Bundling Strategy

  • Talkpush Full Suite Discount: 20% discount for clients using Attract + Conversational AI + Onboarding + TalkLearn
  • Migration Incentive: First 3 months at Starter pricing for Enterprise volume to encourage adoption
  • Template Library Add-On: Pre-built industry compliance modules available as add-on ($500-2,000/month)
